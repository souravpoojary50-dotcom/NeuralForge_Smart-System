# NeuralForge_Smart-System
🇮🇳 Unified Public Service Workflow Platform

📌 Problem Statement

India has powerful digital systems like DigiLocker and UPI. However, citizens still face:

- Repeated document submissions across departments
- Lack of transparency in approval processes
- Multiple office visits
- Slow coordination between government departments

This leads to delays, inefficiency, and frustration for citizens.

---

💡 Solution

We propose a Unified Public Service Workflow Platform where:

- Citizens submit their documents once
- Documents can be reused securely across services
- Payments are handled seamlessly via UPI
- Users can track their request end-to-end

---

🚀 Key Features

- 🔐 Single Document Submission
  Upload documents once and reuse across services

- 💳 Integrated UPI Payments
  Pay service fees directly within the platform

- 📊 Real-Time Tracking
  Track application status from submission to approval

- 🏢 Department Integration (Simulation)
  Simulates coordination between multiple departments

- 🔔 Notifications & Updates
  Alerts for approvals, rejections, and pending actions
